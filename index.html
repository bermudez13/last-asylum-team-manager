Army Selection Logic

The app ranks all players by Team Might from highest to lowest. The top 45 players are selected for battle. Ranks 1–30 are split between Army 1 and Army 2 using a balanced pattern, while ranks 31–45 are assigned to Army 3. Any player ranked 46 or lower is marked as Backup.

The goal is to create two strong armies to compete for two flags, while the third army uses the weakest 15 players from the selected top 45.

Army Organization Strategy

Balanced mode is recommended because Canyon Conquest is won by taking 2 out of 3 flags. Instead of concentrating all power into one army, this strategy spreads the top 30 players between Army 1 and Army 2 to reduce overkill and increase the chance of winning two lanes. Army 3 receives ranks 31–45 as the weaker support army.
